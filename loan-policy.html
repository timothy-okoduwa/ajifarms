Loan Policy
At Ajifarms we offers loan services to eligible users of our mobile application. This Loan Policy outlines the terms and conditions that govern the provision of loans through Ajifarms. By applying for and accepting a loan through our app, you agree to comply with the following terms:
1. Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for a loan through Ajifarms, you must meet the following criteria:
a. You must be a registered user of Ajifarms.
b. You must provide accurate and complete information during the loan application process.
c. You must satisfy the creditworthiness requirements determined by us, which may include credit history and financial stability assessments.
d. You must be of legal age in your jurisdiction to enter into a loan agreement.
2. Loan Application
a. Loan Application Process: To apply for a loan, you must follow the loan application process provided within the Ajifarms app. This process may require you to provide information about your financial status, employment, and other relevant details.
b. Accuracy of Information: You must ensure that all information provided in your loan application is accurate and up-to-date.
3. Loan Approval and Disbursement
a. Loan Approval: Loan approval is at our discretion, and it is based on a thorough assessment of your application. We may perform credit checks and review your financial history.
b. Loan Amount: The approved loan amount may vary based on your eligibility, financial stability, and other factors determined by us.
c. Disbursement: Once your loan application is approved, we will disburse the loan amount to your designated account within the Ajifarms app
4. Loan Repayment
a. Loan Repayment Terms: The terms and conditions of your loan repayment, including interest rates, repayment schedule, and the total repayment amount, will be specified in the loan agreement provided to you.
b. Repayment Schedule: You must adhere to the specified repayment schedule to ensure timely repayment of the loan.
c. Default and Late Payments: Failure to make timely repayments may result in late fees, increased interest, and potential default consequences, as outlined in the loan agreement.
5. Prepayment and Early Repayment
You may have the option to make prepayments or early repayments, depending on the terms and conditions specified in your loan agreement. Any associated fees or conditions will be detailed in the agreement.
6. Loan Termination
a. Termination by You: You have the right to repay the loan in full at any time, in accordance with the terms of the loan agreement.
b. Termination by Us: We reserve the right to terminate the loan agreement in the event of default, fraud, or any violation of the terms and conditions set forth in this policy.
7. Privacy and Data Security
All information collected during the loan application and disbursement process will be handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
8. Contact Us
If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@ajifarms.com
By applying for a loan through Ajifarms, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this Loan Policy. Failure to comply with these terms may result in financial consequences and actions in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
